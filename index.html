About Sina Cafe

Your gateway to authentic Ethiopian flavors in the heart of Frankfurt

Welcome to Sina Cafe, where tradition meets taste in Frankfurt's vibrant culinary scene. Located on Schloßstraße 125, we bring you the rich, authentic flavors of Ethiopian cuisine prepared with love and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our skilled chefs use only the finest spices and ingredients to create dishes that transport you straight to the heart of Ethiopia. From our signature Chechebsa to our flavorful vegetarian options, every meal is a celebration of Ethiopian culture and hospitality.

In addition to our cafe services, we have a delicious restaurant located at Werftstraße 15, 60327 Frankfurt am Main. Experience the warmth of Ethiopian dining traditions in our cozy restaurant, where every guest is treated like family. Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey that awakens your senses and nourishes your soul.
